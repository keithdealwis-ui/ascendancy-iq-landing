FAQs

Frequently Asked
Questions

How is this different from ChatGPT or Copilot?

ChatGPT and Copilot are assistants — they wait for you to prompt them. Ascendancy IQ agents are autonomous collaborators. They take a brief, break it down, coordinate with each other, and deliver complete solutions. Think of it as the difference between having a spell-checker and having a writing team.

What tech stacks do the agents support?

Our Engineering Agent is proficient in React, Vue, Next.js, Node.js, Python, Go, Ruby, and most modern frameworks. The Design Agent outputs production-ready Tailwind CSS, styled-components, or vanilla CSS based on your preference. If you have a specific stack, we'll adapt.

Do I need technical knowledge to use this?

Basic technical literacy helps, but you don't need to be a developer. The PM Agent translates your business requirements into technical specs. That said, you should be able to review code and understand what you're deploying. We provide guidance every step of the way.

What happens to my code if I cancel?

Everything the agents create goes directly into your repository. You own 100% of the IP. If you cancel, you keep everything — there's no vendor lock-in, no proprietary formats, no hidden dependencies. Your code is your code.

How do agent hours work?

Agent hours measure actual computation time, not wall-clock time. When agents work in parallel, they can accomplish in 1 hour what would take a human team days. Most Solo users find 50 hours handles 2-3 significant features per month. SMB users typically ship entire products.
